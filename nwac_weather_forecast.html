NWAC Weather Synopsis Issued: 2:10 PM PST Sunday, February 2, 2025 by Dennis D'Amico

Weather Synopsis

Low and mid-level clouds have become more widespread throughout the mountains as snow shower activity has begun to ramp up for the Olympics, Mount Hood, the western slopes of the Cascades, and mountain passes. With cold temperatures in place, expect a few inches of cold and fluffy snow tonight, with larger amounts possible locally, especially near Mount Baker and Rainier. Outside of the northwest Cascades including Mt Baker, snow showers taper overnight as east flow and Fraser outflow filter in, dropping the temperatures even further.
