Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
432 PM PST Tue Feb 18 2025

An upper ridge over the region will move inland tonight, allowing for a warm front to move into western Washington. Lowland rain and mountain snow are expected across Mount Rainier National Park as a trailing front moves across the region on Wednesday. Precipitation chances will continue Thursday and Friday at the park as another series of fronts moves through the region. An atmospheric river looks to move into the area over the weekend, bringing heavy rain and snow to the area into early next week.
