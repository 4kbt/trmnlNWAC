Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
248 PM PST Tue Dec 9 2025

A strong atmospheric system will continue to bring periods of heavy rain and gusty winds to western Washington through Wednesday, especially at Mount Rainier National Park.
