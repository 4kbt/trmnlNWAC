Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
354 AM PDT Wed Jun 18 2025

A weak frontal system will move through the area today, bringing showers to Mount Rainier National Park, as well as strong winds to the summit of the mountain. A cooler system moving into the region Friday into the weekend will bring more widespread precipitation to the park and will bring snow levels down to 5000-6000 ft. Conditions will generally trend warmer across the park by early next week, but chances of showers will linger at times.
