Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
400 PM PST Thu Jan 23 2025

A weak front will bring in clouds and light snow over higher elevations of the Park tonight. High pressure will rebound over the weekend and into early next week, resulting in prolonged cooler temperatures, dry conditions, and periods of morning fog. A pattern change is on track for the latter half of next week, with troughing expected to bring precipitation to western Washington at the end of the month.
