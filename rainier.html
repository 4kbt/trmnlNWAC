Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
316 PM PDT Tue Sep 30 2025

A frontal system will move through the area Tuesday night bringing rain, high elevation snow and locally windy conditions. An unstable air mass behind the front will lead to showers and isolated thunderstorms on Wednesday. A weakening trough over the region will keep conditions unsettled into Friday. A trend toward drier weather will begin over the weekend as a ridge of high pressure aloft builds offshore.
