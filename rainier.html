Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
244 AM PST Tue Jan 7 2025

High pressure will build over the region throughout the week, with a weak front moving through western Washington late Tuesday bringing in breezy conditions over higher elevations of the Park. A more organized system will move through on Friday, bringing a brief shot of low elevation rain and mountain snow to the region. Dry conditions are on track to return by the end of the weekend as high pressure rebuilds.
