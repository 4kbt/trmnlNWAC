Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
347 AM PDT Sat Mar 29 2025

A trough of low pressure over the area will weaken on Saturday allowing showers to diminish across the region. A weak ridge of high pressure will provide drier and mild conditions on Sunday before another trough arrives early in the coming week with additional chances for showers and a return to cooler conditions. Drier and somewhat warmer conditions are expected to return at the end of next week as high pressure rebuilds into Western Washington.
