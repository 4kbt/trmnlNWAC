Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
220 AM PST Thu Nov 20 2025

A splitting frontal system will maintain light showers across western Washington this morning, with showers along the coast throughout the day. High pressure will allow much of the region to dry out on Friday and Saturday, with chances for additional rain over the northern interior. A stronger system will bring more substantial chances for rain and mountain snow across western Washington Sunday into early next week.
