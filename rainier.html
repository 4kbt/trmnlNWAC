Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
307 PM PST Mon Feb 3 2025

An upper level low will continue to produce snow showers at times over the mountain through the week, with the best chance for accumulating snow Monday night into Tuesday, and Wednesday. Temperatures will get chilly through the week, especially towards the Summit and Camp Muir areas with temperatures remaining around or below zero. Gusty winds are also expected at higher elevations.
