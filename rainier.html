Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
313 PM PST Tue Dec 16 2025

A period of very active weather will dominate the week ahead as a series of frontal systems produce cascading impacts across Western Washington. Another period of windy conditions is expected tonight into early Wednesday at Mount Rainier National Park. Snow levels fall down to 2000 to 2500 feet by early Wednesday morning with heavy snowfall expected in the mountains, with a blizzard warning out for Mount Rainier National Park. Cool, unsettled conditions will remain in place through the remainder of the week with substantial additional snowfall in the mountains.
