Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
407 AM PST Fri Feb 7 2025

Temperatures will be cold Friday into the weekend, especially towards the Summit and Camp Muir where temperatures are likely to remain near to below zero. A weak system moving across the region tonight into Saturday will maintain chances of snow showers across Mount Rainier National Park. Showery weather will continue through Sunday. Drier conditions and very cold temperatures are expected Monday night into Wednesday, before chances for snow showers increase again by Wednesday night.
