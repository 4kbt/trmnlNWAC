Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
353 AM PDT Mon Oct 13 2025

Showers will gradually taper across Mount Rainier National Park today as a weather system slowly exits the area. Drier conditions will return to the park on Tuesday and persist into Wednesday with offshore flow. Showers will return to the park Thursday evening into Friday as a weak weather system moves into the region, with a stronger, wetter system slated to arrive over the weekend.
