Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
318 PM PDT Fri Sep 26 2025

A mostly clear weekend is in store, at least until Sunday afternoon when rain begins to fill in across Puget Sound. Some morning clouds are expected as a result of zonal flow continuing across western Washington. Rain will arrive in the park late Sunday afternoon and wet conditions will persist throughout the week. Snow is expected at elevations above 8500 feet.
