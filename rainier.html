Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
422 AM PDT Mon Sep 8 2025

A weather system moving over the Pacific Northwest will maintain cool, cloudy, and showery conditions at Mount Rainier National Park through much of the week. Thunderstorms will be possible at the park at times, mainly during the afternoon and evening hours. Another system moving into the region next weekend will bring the potential for even wetter conditions to the park.
