Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
304 PM PDT Mon May 12 2025

Lingering moisture with an upper low passing to the south will keep scattered showers and possibly an isolated afternoon thunderstorm in the forecast at the park today. Upper level ridging will start to build into the area midweek for the return of drier conditions at the park. A broad upper level trough will bring active weather back to the area for the end of the week.
