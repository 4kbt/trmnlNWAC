Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
1246 PM PST Mon Dec 1 2025

Generally quiet weather is expected through Wednesday night or Thursday though strong winds near the summit will create challenging conditions. The door will open to a series of weather systems beginning Thursday and continuing into next week. Initially the concerns will be gusty winds and high elevation snow then transition to a river flood threat with strong winds this weekend into next week. Active weather is likely to continue through at least the first half of next week.
