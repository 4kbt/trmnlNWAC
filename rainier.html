Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
333 AM PST Wed Feb 19 2025

A frontal system will cross Mount Rainier National Park today and tonight, maintaining rain and snow and gusty winds. A weak upper ridge will build into the region Thursday, but will only be a brief break with another series of fronts approaching Friday. An atmospheric river looks to arrive this weekend, bringing warmer conditions, heavier precipitation, and rising snow levels across the park.
