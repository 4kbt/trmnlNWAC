Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
258 PM PDT Sat Oct 4 2025

Skies will begin to clear tonight as upper level ridging begins to build over Mount Rainier National Park behind an exiting shortwave trough. This will allow for more clear skies as well as warmer temperatures and lower humidity through Tuesday. Cooler and wetter conditions look to return Wednesday through the latter half of the week, but details remain uncertain.
