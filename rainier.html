Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
234 PM PDT Tue Jul 22 2025

Mid level trough of low pressure will continue to exit east of the area tonight. High pressure will nose into the area Wednesday with a brief warming trend. But by later the week a more zonal type flow will be present then trending towards more approaching troughing by Friday and Saturday. So warmer than normal temperatures mid week will give way to near normal conditions through the weekend with rising heights to return early next week for the next warmup.
