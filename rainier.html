Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
251 AM PDT Sat Sep 20 2025

Mostly dry and mild conditions continue today at Mount Rainier National Park before a frontal system will move across the park early Sunday morning, bringing widespread rain, breezy winds, and cooler temperatures. High pressure will return Monday for a return to sunny skies and warmer conditions through at least the first half of the week.
