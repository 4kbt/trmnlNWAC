Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
423 PM PST Thu Dec 25 2025

A frontal system lifting across the region will maintain showers across Mount Rainier National Park tonight. Another weather system will move across the area on Friday, bringing another round of showers to the park and strong winds to higher elevations of the mountain. Conditions will dry out over the weekend and will persist into early next week as a ridge of high pressure builds over the Pacific Northwest. Chances of showers return to the park near midweek.
