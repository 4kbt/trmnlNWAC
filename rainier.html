Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
415 AM PDT Wed Sep 24 2025

One more dry and mild day across Mount Rainier National Park with high pressure in place over the region. Expect continued smoke from nearby fires to remain today. Stronger onshore flow will bring cooler conditions, increased clouds, and stronger winds for the upper elevations of the mountain later this week. A more significant weather system likely arrives late in the weekend with a return of rain and snow to the park.
