Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
232 PM PDT Sun Jun 21 2026

Warm and dry conditions under strong high pressure will move into the park area for the first half of the week. If hiking or camping in the lower elevations of the mountain, stay hydrated and wear loose fitting clothing to prevent the risk of heat related illness. Cooler air will move into the region mid to late week as onshore flow increases. A front Friday into Saturday will bring the chance of measurable precipitation to the park.
