Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
412 PM PDT Wed Jul 22 2026

Hot temperatures will continue across Mount Rainier National Park into this evening with an upper level ridge over western Washington. Chances of showers and isolated thunderstorms will increase across the park tonight into Thursday as moisture moves up into the area from the south. A weather system will push inland over the weekend, bringing cooler temperatures and additional chances of showers to the park. Cooler temperatures will then persist through the first half of next week.
