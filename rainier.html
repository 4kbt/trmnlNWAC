Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
427 AM PST Sun Nov 30 2025

Cooler and drier conditions are expected at Mount Rainier National Park today into Monday. A weak disturbance moving across the region Monday afternoon into Tuesday will bring some light rain and snow to the park. Drier conditions are expected at the park again on Wednesday, before wetter and stronger systems move into the area from Thursday onward.
