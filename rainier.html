Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
128 PM PDT Tue Aug 5 2025

A frontal system will move across the region tonight through early Thursday, bringing rain chances to western Washington. Snow levels will fall below the summit during this period. Thunderstorms will be possible, mainly Wednesday afternoon/evening. Conditions will generally remain cooler and cloudier through Thursday as upper level troughing continues to influence the Pacific Northwest.
