Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
422 AM PST Mon Dec 30 2024

Shower activity will continue across Mount Rainier National Park today before tapering tonight. Drier conditions are expected at the park on Tuesday before another system moves into the region on Wednesday and brings additional precipitation and strong winds to the summit of the mountain. An active weather pattern will persist late in the week and through the weekend as additional systems move through the region.
