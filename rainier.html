Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
1255 PM PST Mon Dec 9 2024

A ridge of high pressure will move across the region through early Wednesday allowing for mostly dry but cool conditions. Overnight fog, dense in areas, is expected tonight along with areas of frost and perhaps black ice. Additional wet weather systems will begin to move through the area Wednesday and beyond with the strongest system likely on Saturday. That system could bring gusty winds and a return of heavy mountain snowfall.
