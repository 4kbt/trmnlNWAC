Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
406 PM PDT Sat Mar 29 2025

Weakening low pressure over the area will continue to lead to diminishing showers across Mount Rainier National Park tonight. Temporary weak high pressure will provide drier and mild conditions on Sunday across the park. Another disturbance arrives early Monday for showers and a return to cooler conditions through much of the week ahead. Drier and somewhat warmer conditions then return as high pressure rebuilds late next week.
