Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
319 AM PDT Sun May 4 2025

A drier trend will commence across Mount Rainier National Park today and persist through the first half of the week as an upper level ridge builds back into the area. Winds near the summit will be strong at times tonight and will ease again into Monday. Chances of showers will increase across the park on Wednesday afternoon as a weak system approaches the area. Drier conditions then return Thursday and will persist into next weekend.
