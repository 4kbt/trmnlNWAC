Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
531 AM PST Tue Feb 25 2025

Strong winds and heavy mountain snow will continue at Mount Rainier National Park today as a low pressure system moves inland across western Washington. Conditions will dry out heading into Wednesday as high pressure builds back into the area. Shower chances return Thursday, with drier conditions expected again on Friday. Showers will be possible over the weekend as additional systems move into the region.
