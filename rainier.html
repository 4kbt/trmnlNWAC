Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
326 PM PST Sun Dec 29 2024

Showers persist behind the frontal passage tonight into Monday, bringing light lowland rain and snow above 2500 ft to Mount Rainier National Park. Showers will linger into Monday, however expect a gradual drying trend to commence and persist into Tuesday for a brief break in the active weather pattern. The active weather then returns mid to late week as additional systems with more warmth and moisture move into the region.
