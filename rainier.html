Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
400 PM PDT Wed Aug 6 2025

A cooler weather system will cross Mount Rainier National Park this evening, spreading showers and perhaps an isolated thunderstorm as snow levels fall below Camp Muir. Cooler and cloudier conditions will linger into Thursday, with showers tapering during the day. Warmer and drier conditions will develop at the park on Friday and persist into early next week as high pressure returns to the region.
