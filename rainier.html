Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
322 AM PDT Sat Jun 20 2026

High pressure aloft will briefly weaken over Western Washington on Saturday as an upper trough moves across British Columbia. Upper level ridging will rebuild into the region Sunday through Tuesday for a significant warming trend. The ridge will begin to weaken by the middle of the coming week for cooler conditions. A frontal system could bring the regions first chance of rain in more than two weeks by the end of next week.
