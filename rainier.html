Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
400 PM PST Fri Feb 21 2025

An atmospheric river will set up over Western Washington making for a very wet weekend. The river will move out of the area Sunday night. After a brief break early Monday a front will move through later Monday into early Tuesday for another round of rain and snow at the higher elevations. An upper level ridge will try and build in the middle of next week but systems nearby will keep a chance of precipitation at times Wednesday through Friday.
