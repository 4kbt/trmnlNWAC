Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
430 AM PST Fri Jan 3 2025

A pair of weather systems will move through the park Friday and Saturday. The first one will move through Friday afternoon. The second one Saturday night. A third system will nick the park Sunday night into Monday morning. An upper level ridge will build later Monday with the ridge remaining in place through the middle of next week. The ridge might not be strong enough to prevent weak systems from brushing the park.
