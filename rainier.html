Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
304 PM PST Fri Feb 28 2025

An upper level ridge will maintain warm and dry conditions over Mount Rainier National Park through Saturday. An upper level low will move in Sunday, bringing a return to cooler and cloudy conditions and well as chances for scattered showers into early next week at the park. Drier conditions look to temporarily return mid to late next week.
