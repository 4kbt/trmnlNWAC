Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
435 AM PST Sat Dec 28 2024

A series of weather systems moving across western Washington will bring heavy snow and strong winds to Mount Rainier National Park at times over the weekend. The heaviest snow accumulations are expected Sunday night into Monday. A brief break in the weather will be possible on Tuesday, before additional systems move into the region mid to late week and bring additional rounds of breezy winds and heavy precipitation.
