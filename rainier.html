Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
313 PM PDT Sun May 11 2025

A frontal system will cross over western Washington resulting in widespread showers at Mount Rainier National Park. Lingering moisture will keep scattered showers in the forecast for Monday at the park. Upper level ridging will start to build into the area midweek for the return drier conditions at the park. Active weather is possible for late in the week.
