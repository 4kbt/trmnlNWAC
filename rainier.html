Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
413 PM PST Mon Dec 30 2024

Shower activity will continue across Mount Rainier National Park this afternoon as an upper level trough swings across the area. Showers will then gradually taper tonight for a drier day on Tuesday. The active weather pattern returns mid to late week as a series of systems moves through the region and brings additional rounds of lowland rain, mountain snow, and breezy conditions.
