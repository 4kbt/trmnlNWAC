Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast...RESENT
National Weather Service Seattle WA
345 AM PDT Thu Jun 12 2025

A weak upper trough will move into Western Washington on Thursday then remain over the area on Friday. This will lead to cooler temperatures and increased cloud cover at times in the National Park. Upper ridging will weakly rebuild late in the weekend for a modest warming trend before another trough approaches the area early in the coming week.
