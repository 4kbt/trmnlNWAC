Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
349 AM PST Sun Dec 21 2025

A series of weather systems will move across western Washington over the next few days, bringing periods of heavy mountain snow to Mount Rainier National Park. Strong winds are also expected at times through the first part of the week across the higher reaches of the mountain. A few weaker systems then look to move up into the area mid to late week, bringing rain and snow showers to the park at times.
