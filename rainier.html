Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
357 AM PDT Tue Oct 14 2025

Weak high pressure over the region will keep conditions cool and dry at Mount Rainier National Park today and Wednesday. Strong winds are expected near the summit today. Showers will return Thursday into Friday as a weak system moves into the region. A stronger, wetter system moving into the area over the weekend will bring gusty winds and additional rounds of precipitation, with snow levels falling to around 4500 ft by Sunday night.
