Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
530 AM PST Thu Feb 6 2025

An upper level low spinning offshore will continue to bring chances of snow showers to Mount Rainier National Park this morning. Temperatures will be cold Friday into the weekend, especially towards the Summit and Camp Muir where temperatures are likely to remain near to below zero. A weak system moving across the region over the weekend will maintain chances of snow showers at the park. Cold and showery weather will continue into next week.
