Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
428 AM PST Sat Feb 22 2025

Expect gusty winds, heavy rain and snow, and rising snow levels across Mount Rainier National Park Saturday and Sunday with an atmospheric river in place over the region.
