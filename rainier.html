Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
350 AM PDT Tue Jun 17 2025

Shower chances will increase across Mount Rainier National Park tonight into Wednesday as a weak system moves across the area. Strong winds are expected to develop near the summit of the mountain tonight and Wednesday. A cooler system moving into the region Friday into the weekend will bring additional shower chances to the park and will bring snow levels down to 5000-6000 ft. Showers look to linger across the park into early next week.
