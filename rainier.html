Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
130 AM PDT Wed Jul 30 2025

A warm and unstable air pattern over the Cascades will produce the chance of showers and thunderstorms this afternoon and Thursday afternoon. The Thursday afternoon storms will have greater coverage if storms initiate. Lightning, gusty winds, downpours are expected to be the primary hazards.
