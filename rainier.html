Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
242 AM PDT Thu Oct 30 2025

A weak ridge of high pressure moving over the region will bring drier conditions to Mount Rainier National Park through today. An atmospheric river will then move into the area Friday into Saturday bringing higher snow levels, gusty winds, moderate to heavy rain, and high elevation snow. Showers will taper on Sunday, however additional systems look to keep conditions unsettled across the park through early next week.
