Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
425 PM PDT Tue Oct 28 2025

A weather system will move across the region tonight into Wednesday, bringing wet and windy conditions to Mount Rainer National Park. A break in the weather is expected on Thursday, before an atmospheric river moves into the area Friday. This system will bring moderate to heavy rainfall, windy conditions, and higher snow levels to the park. Showers will gradually taper through the day on Sunday. Additional, weaker systems may move into the region early next week for continued unsettled conditions.
