Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
357 PM PDT Mon Oct 20 2025

A weak ridge will bring drier conditions to the National Park through Tuesday. Another frontal system will reach the area on Wednesday with mainly light precipitation. A stronger frontal system at the end of the week will produce rain and high mountain snow. A cool upper trough will lower snow levels this weekend with the potential of significant early season snowfall.
