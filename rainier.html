Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
335 AM PDT Thu Oct 16 2025

The next weather system will move across the area tonight into Friday, bringing chances of showers to Mount Rainier National Park and strong winds to higher elevations of the mountain. A stronger, wetter system will follow over the weekend, bringing another round of strong winds and precipitation to the park. Snow levels will fall to around 5000 ft by Sunday night. Unsettled conditions look to persist at the park into early next week.
