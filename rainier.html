Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
222 PM PDT Thu Jul 2 2026

Showers will continue to move across the park tonight through Friday morning with a weakening front. The area will become dry over the 4th of July weekend, with breezy winds at the higher elevations and the east slopes. There will be warmer temperatures going into Monday, with a slight cooldown midweek as another weak front may try to produce a couple of showers along the Cascades.
