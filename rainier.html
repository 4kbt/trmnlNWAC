Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
455 PM PST Sat Dec 13 2025

Drier conditions will persist across Mount Rainier tonight, before a weak system crosses the region on Sunday for another round of rain and snow. A stronger system will follow Monday into Tuesday, bringing heavier precipitation and strong winds for the higher elevations of the mountain. Snow levels fall Tuesday and Wednesday, with heavy snow expected at the park above Longmire. Periods of rain and snow will then persist through the remainder of the next week.
