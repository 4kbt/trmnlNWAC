Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
302 PM PDT Sun Jun 8 2025

Upper level ridge over Mount Rainier National Park will bring warmer temperatures through Monday. The ridge will weaken Monday and exit the region on Tuesday. Temperatures will gradually cool down each day starting Monday night. A trough will approach the coast on Wednesday and settle over the region through the weekend. Conditions expected to remain dry into the weekend.
