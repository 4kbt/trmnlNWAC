Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
507 AM PDT Thu Mar 20 2025

Scattered showers will continue across Mount Rainier National Park this morning before another system moves into the region tonight and brings the next round of lowland rain, heavy mountain snow, and strong winds to the area. The pattern looks to remain active through the weekend, with the potential for an atmospheric river to set up over the region on Sunday, which would result in much higher snow levels across the park. High pressure building over the region Tuesday will bring drier and warmer conditions to the region.
