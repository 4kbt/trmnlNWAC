Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
456 AM PST Tue Feb 4 2025

An upper level low spinning across the Pacific Northwest will continue to bring chances of snow showers to Mount Rainier National Park over the next few days. Temperatures will be cold through the week, especially towards the Summit and Camp Muir where temperatures are likely to remain at or below zero. Gusty winds are expected at higher elevations.
