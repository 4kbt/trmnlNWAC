Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
313 PM PST Sun Nov 16 2025

Showers will continue across the region this afternoon through Tuesday as a couple of troughs pass through the region. Snow levels will drop over the mountain Tuesday. Drier weather is expected late Tuesday into Wednesday as a ridge briefly builds over the region, giving a small chance for the first peaks of sunshine in a short while. Rain chances will increase again going towards the end of the week, with near normal temperatures favored.
