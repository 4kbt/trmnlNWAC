Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
301 AM PST Sat Jan 10 2026

Another frontal system will lead to periods of moderate to locally heavy rain at times Sunday into Monday along with snow levels rising above 6000 feet. Strong winds are expected at Camp Muir and the summit. Drier conditions are favored to return by the middle of next week as high pressure aloft rebuilds into the region.
