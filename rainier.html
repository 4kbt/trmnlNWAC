Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
132 PM PDT Sun Aug 10 2025

High pressure will continue to build into the region through early this week, bringing dry conditions and well above normal temperatures. Widespread Moderate HeatRisk will continue across the lowlands through Tuesday, and a Heat Advisory remains in effect across the lowlands and mountain valleys. An upper trough will move into the area towards the second half of next week, bringing in cooler and cloudier conditions with increasing chances for precipitation, particularly late in the week into next weekend.
