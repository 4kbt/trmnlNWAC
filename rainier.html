Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
347 AM PST Sun Dec 14 2025

A weak system crosses Mount Rainier National Park on Sunday for another round of rain and snow. A stronger system will follow Monday into Tuesday, bringing heavier precipitation and strong winds for the higher elevations of the mountain. Snow levels fall Tuesday and Wednesday, with heavy snow expected at the park above Longmire. Periods of rain and snow will then persist through the remainder of the next week.
