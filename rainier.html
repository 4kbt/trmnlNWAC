Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
406 AM PDT Mon Sep 29 2025

A frontal system will push through Mount Rainier National Park today. A stronger frontal system is expected Tuesday through Wednesday. These systems will bring widespread precipitation, breezy to gusty winds, and a slight chance of thunder. Unsettled conditions are favored to continue late into the week as additional systems move into the region.
