National Weather Service Seattle WA
419 PM PDT Tue Oct 7 2025

Upper level trough moving south along the British Columbia coast will turn into an upper level low as it reaches the offshore waters of the Washington coast Wednesday afternoon. The low will wobble around off the coast before weakening to a trough and moving inland Friday night. A cooler upper level low will drop out of British Columbia and move over the park for the weekend into the first part of next week. Snow levels down to near 4500 feet Sunday and Monday.
