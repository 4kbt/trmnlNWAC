Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
221 PM PST Fri Dec 27 2024

An active weather pattern will continue across Western Washington the next few days, but with no significant high impact weather expected at this time. Increased mid level heights through Saturday suggest warmer than normal temperatures, but this will give way to temperatures dropping back to near normal for Sunday- Tuesday. This will be as a trough brings lower heights to the area. However after Tuesday the trend is for increasing heights as ridging looks to develop over the Western US. This will favor warmer than normal temperatures into the start of the New Year.
