Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
241 PM PST Mon Jan 27 2025

Chilly but dry conditions will persist through mid- week. After a long dry spell, a wetter and slightly warmer system will impact the region late Thursday through Friday bringing a return of lowland rain and mountain snow. Cooler conditions are favored to gradually return Saturday into next week and combined with light precipitation could bring a change from rain to snow to the western Washington lowlands.
