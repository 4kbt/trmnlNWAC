Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
356 AM PDT Sat Oct 4 2025

A shortwave disturbance will allow for a chance for scattered showers before dry conditions return to to the park Sunday. Upper level ridging will strengthen over the Pacific and slowly move eastward into the first half of next week, warming temperatures over the area to above seasonal normals. More active weather is possible for the second half of next week.
