Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
447 AM PST Wed Feb 5 2025

An upper level low spinning across the Pacific Northwest will continue to bring chances of snow showers to Mount Rainier National Park through Friday. Temperatures will be cold throughout the week, especially towards the Summit and Camp Muir where temperatures are likely to remain at or below zero. Gusty winds are expected at higher elevations today. A weak system moving across the region over the weekend will maintain chances of snow showers at the park into early next week.
