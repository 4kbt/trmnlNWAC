Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
237 PM PDT Sat Oct 18 2025

A front will move through the area tonight, bringing rain and snow showers to Mount Rainier National Park. Winds will be gusty at Camp Muir and the Summit as this front moves through. Showers will linger through Sunday with conditions becoming dry on Monday and Tuesday. Additional systems will move through the park starting midweek through the weekend, bringing more rain and more snow to the higher elevations of the park.
