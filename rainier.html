Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
527 AM PDT Tue Aug 12 2025

Hot and dry conditions will continue across Mount Rainier National Park today with high pressure over the area. Cooler and cloudier conditions return to the park Wednesday and will continue through the remainder of the week. Increasing chances of more widespread precipitation are likely Friday and Saturday as a weather system moves into the area. Snow levels look to fall below Camp Muir over the weekend for snow accumulations at higher elevations.
