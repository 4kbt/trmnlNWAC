Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
306 AM PDT Sat May 3 2025

Unsettled weather will persist today at Mount Rainier National Park as an upper level trough moves across the region. Winds near the summit will be strong at times today and again on Sunday night. High pressure will then build back into the area on Sunday, bringing drier conditions to the park through early next week. Chances of showers will then increase again by Wednesday night as the next system approaches the park.
