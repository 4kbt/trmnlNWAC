Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
318 AM PST Mon Dec 1 2025

Weak weather systems moving down the backside of an upper level ridge offshore will move through the park the next few days. One will arrive Monday morning with another one later Monday night into Tuesday. A third weak system will arrive Thursday. The ridge offshore will weaken Thursday night opening the door for a series of stronger systems to reach the park Friday into the first part of next week.
