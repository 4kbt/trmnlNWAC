Increasing cloud cover this afternoon across Mount Rainier National Park as the next front approaches. This front crosses the region tonight through early Monday, bringing cooler temperatures and a few showers. High pressure rebuilds Tuesday and Wednesday with another notable warm up. Another front Thursday brings a brief pause and a few showers, but a return of warmer and dry conditions is expected to return late in the week.
