Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
346 AM PDT Fri Jun 26 2026

A weak frontal system will move inland across Western Washington Friday morning with rain and high elevation snow in the park. An upper level trough will then settle over the region Friday night into Saturday maintaining cool and unsettled conditions with some additional high elevation snow in the park. The trough will shift eastward later Sunday allowing drier conditions to develop into Monday. Another system is expected to drop southward into the area around the middle of next week for continued cool temperatures and another chance for showers.
