Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
425 AM PST Wed Feb 26 2025

Dry conditions are expected across Mount Rainier National Park today. A weak frontal system will approach the region late tonight into Thursday, bringing some chances for light showers to the park. Winds on the higher elevations of the mountain will increase this evening into Thursday, becoming strong at times. Conditions then look to dry out again on Friday before chances of precipitation return for the weekend and persist into early next week.
