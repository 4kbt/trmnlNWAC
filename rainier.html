Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
354 AM PDT Wed Aug 6 2025

A frontal system will move across the region today, bringing showers to Mount Rainier National Park. Isolated thunderstorms will be possible this afternoon and evening and snow levels will fall below the summit. Cooler and cloudier conditions will linger into Thursday, with showers tapering throughout the day. Warmer and drier conditions will commence at the park on Friday and persist into early next week as ridging builds back into the region.
