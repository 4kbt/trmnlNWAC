Mount Rainier Recreational Forecast
National Weather Service Seattle WA
423 PM PDT Tue Aug 12 2025

Cooler and cloudier conditions return to Mount Rainier National Park Wednesday and will continue through the remainder of the week. Heavier precipitation and cooler conditions are likely this weekend as a stronger system arrives Friday and Saturday. This will likely bring snow levels falling below Camp Muir over the weekend with some light snow accumulations at the highest park elevations.
